Ping4Pong

Old-School 4-player pong game

Home Page

Intro

Ping4Pong is an old-fashioned ping pong game in which the user competes against three computer-controlled paddles. The rules are simple: If the ball moves past your paddle, you will lose a life. The last player standing wins. The game was written in Java.

Gameplay

All 4 players begin with 5 lives, and if a ball gets past you (you concede a goal), you lose a life. The last player standing wins. Every five seconds, a randomly selected power-up would appear, and once the user collects it, they will temporarily activate that particular power-up. Also, note that a player can only have one power-up active at a time. I came up with five different kinds of power-ups, as follows:

  1. Life : Gives you an extra life.
  2. Grow : Increases the size of your paddle.
  3. Shrink : Decreases the size of your paddle.
  4. Freeze : Prevents you from moving your paddle.
  5. Invincibility : Builds a brick wall behind your paddle.
