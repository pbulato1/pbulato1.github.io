Ski Penguin

Sports/Ski Simulation game

Home Page

Intro

Ski Penguin is an arcade/ski simulation game. The user controls a penguin skiing down a mountain surrounded with beautiful scenery. The goal is to clear as many gates as possible. The game contains two modes: slalom and giant slalom. The game was made as a WPF app using C#.

Gameplay

The user controls a penguin skiing down the slope containing 50 randomly placed gates. The goal is to clear as many gates as possible. Also, bonus points are awarded depending on the accuracy of your turns. There are three levels of accuracy: perfect, good and fair.
